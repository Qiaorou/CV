Mall Hot Food

Capstone Final Year Project

Industry Partner: DP Architects

2020
Worked in a team of five consisting of students from the Engineering and Product Development (EPD) and Architecture and Sustainable Design (ASD) pillars on Mall Hot Food. In alignment with URA's plans to enhance Orchard Road as a lifestyle destination, Mall Hot Food offers a critical take on Outdoor Refreshment Areas (ORAs) along Orchard Road through modular hawker units. The design integrates thermal cooling, spatial programming as well as structural and mechanical systems from seating to enclosure.
